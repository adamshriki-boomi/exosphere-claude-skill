# Content Patterns

The Boomi Product Team Content Guidelines is a collection of UX writing standards to build a high-quality consistent experience. The guidelines are for new and current writers working on Boomi in-app messaging, microcopy and service content for our users. It can help you determine how to say things, when to say it, and where.  The guidelines also help teams align on terms and writing approaches to ensure:

-   A standard voice and tone to build user trust
-   Consistent brand expression
-   Predictability for users on their journey through the site

The goal is to help the writer craft clear and consistent messaging. Think of these guidelines as a roadmap, guiding effective communication through a set of content rules and patterns that keep everybody’s tone, voice and style on the same page.

These Guidelines:

-   Help all members of the team writer better
-   Create effective, scalable content patterns
-   Help build more effective designs
-   Serve as a source of shared knowledge
-   Be the single source of truth

The Boomi Product Team Content Guidelines is a work-in-progress.  Changes to these guidelines are encouraged and questions should be sent to [maryann.reid@boomi.com](mailto:maryann.reid@boomi.com). Use these guidelines with the design guidelines found below.
