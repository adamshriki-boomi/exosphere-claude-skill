# Toast or Banner

#### Purpose

Toasts and Banners are notification components that help communicate messages to users in different situations. A Toast is typically a short-lived, self-dismissing alert to provide quick, lightweight feedback. A Banner is more persistent, often appearing prominently at the top or bottom of the user interface to convey information that may require additional user attention.

#### General Use & Function

***Toasts:***  
• Display smaller-than-modal notifications for task success or errors that resolve quickly  
• Inform users about actions they took that succeeded, without obstructing their workflow  
• Provide a temporary message that resolves automatically

***Banners:***  
• Conspicuous announcements or warnings requiring sustained visibility  
• Display high-level alerts (e.g., system-wide downtime) that remain until user acknowledgment  
• Provide guidance for next steps after an important system event or error message
