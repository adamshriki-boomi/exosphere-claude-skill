# Voice and Tone

## Who You Are:

As Boomi users, you are programmers, architects, optimizers, builders, and innovators. You are a self-driven professional who can master anything and build anything. You are an expert who expects Boomi to be your creative collaborator, partner, and strategic advisor. You also expect us to respect your time with straightforward information. Together, we innovate and explore.

## We are your Expert Guide

We’re here to help you gain confidence in your use of the platform. As a new user, we can help you figure out where to begin. Moving forward as you gain further proficiency of Boomi, we’re just a tap away from explaining new areas of the platform for you by helping you investigate new features, or research new ways you can use Boomi to solve your business objectives!

If you think of a solution to improve your business with Boomi, we’re here to provide expert guidance as you build it, ensuring you are successful from the start! As you gain further proficiency, we want you to get straight to the information you need.

## Attributes

### Empowering

We look to help, support, and encourage you to experiment and explore all the possibilities in the platform. Whether it is breaking down complex topics into simple and concise explanations or lending a helping hand when a field isn’t clear, we remain present. But, we don’t want to get in your way. We want to partner with you, cheer you on, and share in the success you create for your business within our platform!

### Reliable

We want you to trust our platform through consistent messaging and interaction patterns. We use consistent terminology, naming conventions, and structures so you believe us. You should know what to expect and trust that we’ve got your back!

### Relatable

While we may know a lot more than your average user, we are approachable, warm, and welcoming when you need us. Think of us as your coworker in the next desk over who’s been at it a few more years than you — we’re just a tap away from helping you gain confidence in your use of the platform in an encouraging and helpful way (no condescending here).

## Principles

1.  Be straightforward and to the point. We cut to the chase. We value substantive information over fluff.
2.  Meet the user at their level. If they are an introductory user, it’s about using metaphors and explaining things in a way they will understand. If they are an expert, provide them with enough information on the screen so they can get back to work.
3.  Provide the right amount of information at the right time. Progressively disclose information so as to not overwhelm them.
4.  Celebrate the wins.
5.  Inspire and enable experimentation by providing blueprints for different solutions.
6.  Respect our users time and intelligence. Deliver the facts in a way that is easy to consume. Do not talk in a condescending manner.
