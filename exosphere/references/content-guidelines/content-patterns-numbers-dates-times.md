# Numbers Dates Times

#### Purpose

Textual design element typically above a text field or body.

#### Function

Numbers, dates and times of things or places.

#### General Use

For, When

-   For providing universal numbers, dates, times for activity, process, or other functionality
-   When a user needs to know quantity, timelines, or similar details regardless of location

## Examples

**Date Format**

Spell out Month, Day, Year to avoid confusion as different parts of the world write the time in different orders

##### March 31, 2025

**Time Format**

Hours and minutes with no spaces in 24 hour format: 00:00 with at least 4 digits. If digits include seconds, use 00:00:00

##### 13:06:35 EST, 1:06 PST

**Numbers Format**

Write out all numbers related to measurement, quantity, location, unless otherwise noted.

##### For example 1 is 1, 2 is 2, and 15 is 15
