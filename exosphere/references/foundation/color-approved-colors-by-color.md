# Approved colors by color

As Boomi updates our branding standards, the chart below is subject to (sometimes rapid) change. Check back regularly. If the color or usage you're looking for isn't here, consult with your UX team on what to use instead. The following colors have been approved for the following uses:

All colors, sorted white to black, then [ROYGBIV](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ROYGBIV). Palette colors along with their names, hex and rgba codes are shown next to their approved uses in the light and dark themes. Colors are defined using hex codes, unless they are transparent, in which case they are defined using rgba codes
