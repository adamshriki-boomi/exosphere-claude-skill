### Features

-   **Collapse & Expand Navigation**: The menubar can be collapsed or expanded using a double caret icon at the bottom, allowing users to create more space in content areas or focus on content.
    
-   **Adjustable Height**: The left menubar takes 100% of the height of its parent element. You can adjust the parent element's height as per your requirements. In the example below, the parent element has a height of 800px.
