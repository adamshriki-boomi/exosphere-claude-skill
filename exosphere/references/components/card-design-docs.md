# Card

OverviewGuidelinesAccessibility

A card is a container with informative content and CTA's. Cards can contain image/text content and buttons (icon and/or text) that gives a user options for what they can do with the information provided on the card. A card is visually similar to a [Tile](?path=/docs/components-tile-design-docs--overview) but they provide differing functionalities.
